Info:
~~~~~

Indexing line with a milling and drilling station and a U-shaped conveyor belt with two changes of direction.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Conveyor line with a milling and drilling station with four conveyor belts in a U-shaped arrangement. The model is ideally combinable with the training model "3-axis robot with gripper".
